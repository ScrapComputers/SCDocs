---

The Radar Warning Receiver allows you to get the positions of radars that are activley targeting your creation.

---

## Functions

### getTargets

```lua
RadarWarningReceiver.getTargets()
```

Gets all the radar world positions that are currently targeting the creation.

**Returns:**
- [ **[Vec3](https://scrapmechanicdocs.com/docs/Game-Script-Environment/Userdata/Vec3)[]** ] The table of world positions.
